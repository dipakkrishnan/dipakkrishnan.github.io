Health OS

A private, local-first personal health record with an on-demand multidisciplinary review layer. Health OS runs on your own computer: it connects to your health systems through their patient-access FHIR APIs, downloads your records to your device, and preserves them with full provenance — every normalized fact points back to the exact bytes it came from.

How it works

  • Connect: you sign in to your health system's patient portal (for example MyChart) and authorize read-only access. Health OS registers itself as a client on your device; credentials never leave your machine.
  • Sync: your records — labs, medications, conditions, allergies, encounters and more — are stored locally, versioned, and never overwritten.
  • Ask: agent skills assemble grounded, citation-checked context from your record to help you prepare for visits, reconcile medications, investigate trends, and convene cross-specialty review perspectives.

Privacy

There is no Health OS server. Your data moves directly from your health system to your computer and stays there. Optional AI-powered features send selected context to a model API only under your own account, with explicit confirmation each time.
