Health OS — Terms of Use

Last updated: July 14, 2026

What Health OS is

Health OS is open-source software that runs entirely on your own computer. It connects to your health systems using their patient-access APIs and downloads a copy of your own medical records to your device, where you can build a longitudinal timeline, reconcile medications, and prepare questions for your clinicians.

Your data stays yours

Your records are fetched directly from your health system to your computer. The developer of Health OS operates no servers for your health data and cannot receive, store, or access any of it. Access to your records is read-only: Health OS never writes to your medical record.

AI features

Some optional Health OS features analyze your records using a third-party AI model API under an account you control. Before any such feature runs, Health OS tells you what would be sent and to which provider, and asks for your confirmation. Your data is subject to that provider's terms, under your own agreement with them.

Not medical advice

Health OS is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Its outputs are questions and summaries intended to be discussed with your clinicians, who remain the appropriate decision-makers for your care. Never disregard professional medical advice, or delay seeking it, because of anything produced by Health OS. If you think you may have a medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately.
